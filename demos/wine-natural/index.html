What We Believe

Wine is farming.
We sell what we'd drink.

Good Fruit started as a buying club between two friends who were bored of the same ten bottles at every shop in town. We wanted orange wine before it was cool and pét-nat before it was a meme.

Everything on the shelf is farmed without synthetic chemicals. Most is low or no added sulfur. All of it is from producers whose names we know and whose farms we've either visited or plan to.

We don't carry everything. We carry the good stuff, priced fairly. If you want Parker points and a black-label Napa Cab, we'll point you two blocks east. If you want something alive, stay.
