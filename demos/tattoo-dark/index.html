The Studio

We Don’t Do
Walk-Ins.

Iron Moth is appointment-only by design. Every piece starts with a consultation where we understand what you want, what will hold up over time, and what placement will serve the work best.

We turn away work that isn't a good fit — not to be difficult, but because we'd rather do three things brilliantly than fifteen things adequately.

If we're not the right shop for what you're after, we'll tell you and point you somewhere that is.
