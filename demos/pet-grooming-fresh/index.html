Our Story

We treat every pet
like our own.

Pawfect opened in 2018 because we couldn't find a groomer who treated anxious dogs the way we wanted. So we trained, certified, and built the salon we were looking for.

All our groomers are Fear-Free certified, which means every visit is slow, positive, and treat-heavy. No cage dryers. No rushing. No stress.
