Who We Are

Coffee that

We visit the farms we buy from. Every espresso on our menu has a producer's name attached to it. Not because it sounds good — because we actually went there.

Slow Pour opened in 2019 in a converted corner building. We don't do wi-fi passwords, loyalty apps, or seasonal unicorn lattes. Just very good coffee and a place to sit.
