Our Story

30 Years of
Clean Cars &
Happy Neighbors.

Pete and Sandra Morales opened Sunnyside in the spring of 1994 with two bays, one pressure washer, and a lot of optimism. Back then, half their customers were teenagers washing their first cars.

Thirty years later, those teenagers bring their kids. We've washed the same families' cars for three generations — and we still recognize most of them when they pull up.

We added the automated tunnel in 2008 and our water reclaim system in 2015 — but the crew, the prices, and the handshake when you pull out still feel the same.
