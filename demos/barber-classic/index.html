Our Story

Four Decades of
Honest Craft

Old Blade opened its doors in 1987 when master barber Ray Callahan left the old country with one simple conviction: a great haircut changes how a man carries himself.

Three generations later, we still do it the same way — sharp blades, clean razors, a hot towel, and time. No screens on the walls. No music too loud to talk over. Just the craft.

We've cut hair for lawyers, plumbers, grandfathers, and their grandsons. Come in once and you'll understand why regulars have been coming every two weeks for twenty years.
