Our Story

Just Rachel
and a quiet room.

Rachel spent eight years at a high-volume chain salon before she stopped and asked herself: would I bring my dog here? The answer was no.

The Muddy Paw opened in 2016 as a one-dog-at-a-time studio. Same principle still. One groomer, one dog, full attention.

She's Fear-Free certified and takes four continuing education courses a year. The scissors are sharp, the towels are warm, and the treats are always on the counter.
