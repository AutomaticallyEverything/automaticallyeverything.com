Good to Know

Do you do walk-ins?

Not typically. Everything starts with a short consultation so we can understand your idea, discuss placement, and make sure the design will work long-term.

How do I request a design?

Use the form below or email us with your idea, size, and placement. Include references if you have them — Pinterest boards, sketches, photos, whatever helps us understand the vibe.

What’s the deposit?

We take a $100 deposit to hold your booking date. This goes toward the final cost. Rescheduling with 48+ hours notice is no problem.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Our minimum is $150. Pricing depends on size, complexity, placement, and artist. We'll give you a clear quote during the consultation — no surprises.
